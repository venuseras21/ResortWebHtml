THE ROOMS AT Llerin's Private Pool Resort




VILLA ONG 1

Villa Ong 1 is an elegant private pool villa, a fine example of style intertwined with the natural elements of nature and its traditional architecture. ......

The location allows for great privacy while being within close proximity of the urban cities and within reasonable driving distance from Calamba's great sights.




VILLA ONG 2

Villa Ong 2 is like the twin sister of Villa Ong 1 in terms of it's design and architecture, perfect for taking selfies and groupies. ......

With gorgeous designs and indulgent features, these pools take an afternoon swim to another level and brings fun in the summer.




BAHAY KUBO

Bahay Kubo is a private pool villa that would make feel more close with nature. The nipa huts and greenery surroundings will make you feel as if you are in an oasis. ......

Designed to embrace the natural surroundings, the Bahay Kubo resort is renowned for its sense of tranquillity and relaxation. Dipping in the stunning pool is the perfect way to unwind from work
